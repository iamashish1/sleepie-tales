About Sleepie Tales

Sleepie Tales is an innovative storytelling app that creates personalized bedtime stories for your child. Enter your child’s name and gender to see them as the main character in magical adventures.

How to Use the App?

  • Download Sleepie Tales from the App Store.
  • Enter your child’s name and select their gender.
  • Browse and select from a variety of engaging bedtime stories.
  • Coming soon: Generate custom AI-powered stories based on a selected theme!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is Sleepie Tales free to use?
A: Yes, the app offers a selection of free stories. Additional stories may be available as premium content.

Q: Can I customize more aspects of the story?
A: Currently, you can personalize the name and gender of the character. Future updates will allow more customization options.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Mar 2 2025

Welcome to Sleepie Tales! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you use Sleepie Tales, you may provide us with the child's name and gender to personalize stories. We do not require account registration.
  • Usage Data: We don't collect anonymous data on how users interact with the app.
  • AI-Generated Content: If you use the AI-generated stories feature, your chosen themes may be processed to generate content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize bedtime stories based on the child's name and gender.
  • To improve the app and develop new features.
  • To analyze user interactions to enhance the user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

We do not store any personal information permanently. All entered data is used only within the app and not shared with third parties. Any analytics data collected is anonymized and used for app improvements.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools for analytics, AI-generated stories, or cloud storage. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Sleepie Tales is designed for children, but we do not collect personally identifiable information from them. Parents should supervise children's app usage.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can choose not to enter a child’s name and use generic stories.
  • You can contact us to request data deletion if applicable.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support email.
